About this campaign

Help Refugees provides funding, support and volunteers to more than 80 projects across the world. 94% of the funds we raise are directly spent on supporting refugees.

Our work ranges from life-saving emergency to susatinable initiatives providing education, long-term housing and more.

We will always make sure your donations go where they’re most needed. Read more about our work here.

“Help Refugees is one of the unsung heroes of the European refugee crisis, a young grassroots collective that has tried to create a more dynamic form of aid, where even seasoned aid workers admit their traditional models have failed.” – Patrick Kingsley, The Guardian.
